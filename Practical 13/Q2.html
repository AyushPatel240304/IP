Father

  • Putting food on the table and a roof overhead
    Making sure that a child’s basic needs are met is a major expression of love.
    For some parents throughout the world, it’s also one that is not always easy to accomplish.

  • Making sacrifice
    Putting a child’s needs before your own sometimes means putting your wants, needs and desires on the back burner – indefinitely.

  • Providing protection from outside dangers
    Parental responsibility includes always aiming to keep a child safe from harm.

Mother

  • Always being on call
    Parents don’t punch a time clock; they are always on duty.
    Even when a child is grown and living on his or her own, a parent’s job is never done.

  • Teaching life skills
    One day, your child will leave the nest. A parent’s job in raising a child is to prepare the child for that day.

  • Supporting a child’s interests and dreams
    In most cases, if something is important to your child, even if you don’t understand why, it is important that your child has your full, unquestioning support.
